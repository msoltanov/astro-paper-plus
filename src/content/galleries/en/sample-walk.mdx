This is a **sample gallery** shipped with the issue #553
implementation. Three placeholder SVGs demonstrate the PhotoSwipe v5 lightbox,
responsive thumbnail grid, alt text, and captions. Replace the files under `src/content/galleries/_assets/` with your own
images (or change the frontmatter paths) to publish a real gallery.
